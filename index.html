Everything we do, we do to help people lead healthier, happier lives.

Redefining healthcare through personalized, portable and participatory patient engagement.

Partners

We help patients manage their own health by improving their understanding of their disease and providing behavior change tools, peer and professional support.

Providers

We extend health providers reach beyond the clinical setting by providing an online platform where they can engage with patients via online chat or video.

Pharma

Our peer-to-peer communities support people living with the same disease on the same medication with specialist nurse participation. The result: increased medication adherence and improved patient experience.

Insurers

By increasing people's understanding of their health and empowering them to manage it, we reduce doctors visits, hospital admissions and procedures.
